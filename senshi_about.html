Senshi is a Belgium brand founded in 2020, created by Ghent-based designer Isaac.


In this brand I (Isaac) combined two of my favourite topics namely the Japanese (street)culture and design. At some point I decided to share that passion by designing and selling products, Senshi was born.


Our intention is to offer nice, fair and quality garments with avant-garde designs.
